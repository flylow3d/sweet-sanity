The Hometown Stop

Huntertown's Sweet Little Tradition

Since 2014, the little shop in the renovated garage on Lima Road has been where Huntertown celebrates — after the ball game, after the last day of school, or just because it's Tuesday. Grab a cone at the counter, then settle in under the umbrellas outside.

  • Cozy indoor seating & a relaxed umbrella patio
  • Better-than-the-chains prices, hometown service
  • Dairy-free Dole Whip & oat-based scoops
  • Gift cards & a loyalty program for regulars
