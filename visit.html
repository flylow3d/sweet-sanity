Our Story

The Little Shop in the Garage

Back in 2014, two neighbors looked at an empty garage bay on Lima Road and decided Huntertown deserved its own scoop shop. They fixed it up cute — pink, green and white — and Sweet Sanity was born. The town showed up, cones in hand, and never really stopped.

A new generation of owners took the counter in 2023 and made a good thing sweeter: the case grew to 24 flavors, Hawaiian shave ice joined the menu, and Dole Whip became a daily fixture. Same little garage. Same hometown welcome.
